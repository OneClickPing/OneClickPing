Privacy Policy

OneClick Ping · Effective as of 2026-07-18

This privacy policy applies to the OneClick Ping app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by Oleg Khorolskii (collectively, the "Application"). Oleg Khorolskii is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

OneClick Ping is a ping reducer. The Application does not collect, log, or store any personal information when you download and use it. Registration is not required — no name, email address, or phone number is requested.

If the Application is used with an active internet connection, technical protocol data (such as your ephemeral IP address) is transmitted to facilitate network connectivity, but this data is not retained or used for tracking.

The Application processes only the following limited data:

  • Anonymous account identifier. A random in-app ID is generated to activate your subscription and apply optimized routing profiles. It cannot be linked to your real identity.
  • Subscription status. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The Service Provider receives only a confirmation of an active subscription and its expiration date. Your payment details are never visible to the Service Provider.
  • Diagnostics. If you opt in through iOS settings, Apple may share anonymized crash and diagnostics reports. These contain no personal data.

The Application does not log browsing history, traffic destinations, DNS queries, or the content of network traffic.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The Application contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. The only third party involved is:

  • Apple App Store — payment processing and subscription management, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and App Store data practices.

No data is sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers.

What are my opt-out rights?

Since the Application does not collect personal information through normal use, uninstalling it simply removes the Application from your device.

If you contact the Service Provider directly or voluntarily provide information by other means, you may request deletion of that information by contacting qymenawusgw@hotmail.com.

Data Retention

The anonymous account ID and subscription state are kept while your subscription is active and deleted within 30 days after final expiration. No traffic or connection logs are ever written to persistent storage.

Since the Application does not collect personal information through normal use, children's data is not at risk from use of the Application alone. Security

Because the Application does not collect personal data, the risk of personal data exposure is minimal. However, no security system is completely secure. The Service Provider implements reasonable safeguards to protect systems and any data it holds. All traffic between the Application and the Service Provider's servers is encrypted in transit using modern protocols.

Data Breach Notification

Since the Application does not collect personal data through normal use, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal data is minimal. If a breach occurs involving any data you have voluntarily provided, the Service Provider will notify you as required by applicable law.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect?

Open the app and tap the large connect button. The optimized route is applied automatically if your subscription is active.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone, select OneClick Ping, and choose a plan or cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the purchase.

The connection doesn't start

  • Check that your subscription is active (Settings tab in the app).
  • Switch between Wi-Fi and cellular and try again.
  • If your network is restricted, enable the fallback mode in the app settings.
  • Restart the app. If the problem persists, email us with the account ID shown in Settings.
